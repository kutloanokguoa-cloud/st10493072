1. Market Problem

Faster delivery process for clients that are further away. Since we are online store sizes are not always as accurate as we would like. There is also a lack of versatile items for winter and summer. 
 
2. Product / Service Overview

Zee Crochets offers handmade and  thoughtful products  that are personalized to the clients requirements. Products produced are versatile and clients are able to incorporate them in the different outfits through the different seasons. Material used on all the products does not wear off easily. Zee Crochets provides detailed instructions on how to care for the products and a warranty on worn out items before the given time is provided. 

3. Target Market

Zee Crotchets caters for all ages and genders. Their products are mainly dependent on the customers preferences and it is assembled to customize according to their personality.Location is not a hindering factor for Zee Crotchets as they are able to deliver nationally. 

4. Business Model 

The company mainly generated their profits through the products that they sell. Transportation fee is charged and also if the customers prefer a rather more specialised order they are charged accordingly.
 
5. Go-To Market Strategy

 Zee Crotchets has a number of social media platforms that they use to communicated with their customers. They have Instagram, Tiktok and Whatsapp for more personal engagement. As the business continues to grow partnerships with other brands and social media influences will be made , this is to increase the brands visibility.

 Approximately R5000 funding is needed. It will be used to purchase yarn, which is the main component of the crocheted items. These items can include crocheted skirts, cardigans and basically the big items that people like but are maybe unsure if the business can provide. This can be done over a period of about two months, considering the time that pieces or items take.
